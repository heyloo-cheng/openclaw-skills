## Accessibility Considerations

### Contrast Ratios

| Element | Minimum Ratio | Recommended |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Text on Background | 4.5:1 | 7:1 |
| Large Text | 3:1 | 4.5:1 |
| UI Components | 3:1 | 4.5:1 |

### Common Mistakes

1. **Low Contrast Text**: White text on light yellow (#FFF7ED on #FEF08A) - hard to read
2. **Red/Green Combination**: ~8% of men are color blind
3. **Blue/Yellow Confusion**: Difficult for some color blindness types
4. **Small Text**: Below 12px needs higher contrast

## Platform-Specific Color Tips

### WeChat
- Use warm colors for engagement
- Avoid too many dark colors
- Consider light mode primarily

### Xiaohongshu
- Aesthetic pastels work well
- High saturation for food photos
- Consider dark mode support

### Instagram
- Consistent brand colors
- Bold colors for thumbnails
- White space important

### Print
- CMYK color mode
- Test colors on actual print
- Consider paper texture impact
