## Cross-Domain Insights

### Meta-Signal
Every domain shows the same structural failure: **education over-indexes on tool tutorials and under-indexes on systems thinking, decision-making, and production design**. The person who owns "here's how to actually think about this" wins.

### Priority Stack-Rank (Operator Preference-Adjusted)
1. **Programming Education** (tutorial hell, AI-era learning) — deep personal interest, world-class pedagogy background, familiar format from Scotch.io days
2. **DevOps Education** (production design, decision frameworks, platform engineering) — "especially lethal" edge, richest signal density, highest price tolerance
3. **Cybersecurity Education** (career roadmaps, honest paths) — strongest edge but least exciting to operator
4. **Digital Templates** — fastest to money, good signal generator, lower ceiling

### Price Architecture
- Lead magnet / audience builder: $0-19
- Core products: $29-79
- Premium bundles: $99-199
- The sweet spot across all domains: **$29-49 for a single focused product**
